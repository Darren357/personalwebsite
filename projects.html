Hack Button

This is how I won the category "Most Extravagantly Useless" at MLH Local Hack Day 2015. I borrowed an Arduino Mini from a friend, and I made it send a spacebar when two pins were connected. It was called a "hack" button because I had it connected to a laptop with Hacker Typer on the screen.
